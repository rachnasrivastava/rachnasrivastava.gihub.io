About Me

Greetings! I'm a tech enthusiast, author, mom, and a passionate storyteller residing in the beautiful province of Ontario, Canada. I love the vibrant seasons of summer and fall in Canada. There's nothing quite like the joy of crafting stories in my backyard while savoring a cup of ginger tea.
